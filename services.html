01 / Strength Training

Build Raw, Usable Strength

Structured progressive-overload programming on squat, bench, deadlift and accessory lifts. Ideal if your goal is to get measurably stronger, not just tired.

  • 4 Olympic lifting platforms & full rack setup
  • Programmed 4–5 day splits, adjusted monthly
  • Form correction on every session
02 / CrossFit

High-Intensity, Community-Driven

Varied, functional WODs that combine strength, gymnastics and conditioning. Scaled for every level — from first-timers to competitive athletes.

  • Small-group coached classes, max 12 per class
  • Daily WODs posted a week in advance
  • Dedicated functional rig & gymnastics rings
03 / Yoga & Mobility

Recover, Stretch, Reset

Dedicated mobility and yoga sessions to improve flexibility, reduce injury risk and help your body recover between heavy training days.

  • Vinyasa & restorative yoga sessions
  • Guided mobility drills for lifters
  • Air-conditioned dedicated studio
04 / Personal Training

One Coach. One Plan. Zero Guesswork.

One-on-one sessions built entirely around your goals, schedule and injury history — with a coach who tracks every single session.

  • Custom program design & monthly review
  • Flexible scheduling, in-person or hybrid
  • Nutrition guidance included
