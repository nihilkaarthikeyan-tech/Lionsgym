Our Story

The Den That Erode Built

Lions Gym opened its doors in 2014 with a simple belief: fitness sticks when coaching is personal and progress is visible. Twelve years on, the equipment has multiplied and the pride has grown past a thousand strong — but the belief hasn't changed.

We're not a big-box gym where you're a membership number. Every member gets an assessment, a plan, and a coach who actually knows their name and their goals.
