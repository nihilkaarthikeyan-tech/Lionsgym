Questions

Before You Ask

I've never trained before. Can I still join?

That's how most of the pride started. Your first sessions are coached one-on-one until you're confident with the movements — nobody gets thrown to the machines alone.

What are the gym timings?

Sunday 5:30 AM – 10 PM, Monday 5:30 AM – 9 PM, and Tuesday to Saturday 9 AM – 9 PM. Early-bird lifters, the den opens before sunrise twice a week.

Is there a joining fee or lock-in contract?

Neither. You pay for your plan and nothing else, and you can pause, upgrade or leave whenever you want. We keep members with results, not contracts.

How much does membership cost?

Plans start at ₹1,999/month for full floor access, with Pro and Elite tiers adding classes and personal training. Your first session is free either way — see the full comparison.

Do you have a sauna and showers?

Yes — an on-site sauna plus locker rooms with showers, so you can train, recover and head straight to work.
