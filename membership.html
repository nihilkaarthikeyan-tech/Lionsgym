Common Questions

Can I pause my membership?

Yes — pause any plan for up to 30 days a year at no extra cost. Just let the front desk know.

Is there a joining fee?

No hidden joining fees. The price you see is the price you pay, every month.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime — changes apply from your next billing cycle.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

Yes, reach out to our team with valid ID or company details and we'll share applicable discounts.
